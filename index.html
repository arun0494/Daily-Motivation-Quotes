Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

1. Data Collection

This app does not collect any personal data whatsoever. We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, location data, or any other personal information.

2. Local Storage

The "Favorites" feature stores your saved quotes only on your device using the browser's localStorage. This data never leaves your device and is never transmitted to any server.

3. Third-Party Ads

This app may display third-party advertisements. These ad providers may use cookies or similar technologies in accordance with their own privacy policies.

4. No Login Required

This app requires no account, login, or registration of any kind.

5. Children's Privacy

This app does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. The content is suitable for all ages.

6. Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, you may contact us through the app store listing.
